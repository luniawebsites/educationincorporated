School Fees for 2018

Our fees comprise an annual grade-specific tuition fee, IEB charges (for specific testing for grades 6 and 9 as well as the Matric examination fee paid over two years in grades 11 and 12), subject-specific charges (for instance, the drawing equipment required for EGD) and an additional charge for all extras such as curriculum, textbooks, development levy, locker rental, software licences and photographs. There are NO additional compulsory "hidden" charges during the year. The fee you see is the fee you pay – and that's for a private school education in classes with no more than 10 children per teacher. While the additional charges referred to above are payable at the commencement of the year, the others are payable either monthly (over 12 months), termly (we're a three-term school) or annually (a discount does apply to the latter two options).

For families joining us for the first time, there is an initial joining fee payable upon registration. This fee includes all the additional charges listed above. For families with multiple enrolments (e.g. siblings) an additional 5% discount applies to the annual fees of the youngest child enrolled.
