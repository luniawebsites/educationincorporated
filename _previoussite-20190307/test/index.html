Objectives of the Association

The promotion of friendly interaction among all old boys of Drakensberg Boys' Choir School

The promotion of the choir during its tours and at its concerts

The provision of bursaries, scholarships and fee-reduction schemes for pupils, whether related to old boys or otherwise

The raising of funds for the purpose of implementing the objectives contained herein

Generally the furtherance of the interests and prosperity of Drakensberg Boys' Choir School and the Drakensberg Boys' Choir School Community
